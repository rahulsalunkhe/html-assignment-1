Today We are Preparing Chicken Lolipop and the ingridents and receipe is mentioned Below

  • Chicken Breast
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Flour
  • Curd
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Schezwan Sauce

Now below is the Recipe to prepare delicious Chicken Lolipop

  1. Firstly, Clean the Chicken breast with water
  2. Parellely, create a batter of flour, ree chilli powder, salt and eggs
  3. Stir it neatly to make it dense
  4. Then dip the Chicken breasts in it
  5. Alongside, heat the olive to a high temperature
  6. Pour the chicken breasts in the heated oil and deep fry it for 10 mins
  7. Take the Chicken Breast out and garnish the same with Coriander leaves
  8. Serve it with Schezwan Sauce. :)
